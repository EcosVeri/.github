# EcosVeri Brand Notes

## Name

Use `EcosVeri` for the organization and brand. Use lowercase repository names.

## Domains

Primary:

- `ecosveri.dev`

Optional redirect:

- `ecosveri.org`

Future:

- `docs.ecosveri.dev`

## Voice

EcosVeri should sound precise, calm, engineering-led, and evidence-oriented.

Avoid inflated AI claims. Prefer concrete language about retrieval, verification, scientific computing, evaluation, and reproducibility.

## Visual direction

- Minimal geometric `EV` mark.
- High contrast black and white base.
- Amber accent for evidence and review.
- Green accent for verified state.
- Avoid robot, brain, and circuit motifs.
