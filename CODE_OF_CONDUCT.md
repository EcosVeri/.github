# Code of Conduct

EcosVeri is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive open-source community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Make space for careful technical disagreement.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Repeated disruption of discussions after maintainers ask for a change in behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict participation, or ban contributors when behavior harms the community. Reports can be sent privately to project maintainers.
